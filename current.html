Deep Space Network Security

I have received funding from the Ohio Space Grant Consortium to conduct research on network security protocols. I aim to show that the newer WireGuard protocol can offer the same security as IPSec but at a much lower cost in throughput and CPU usage. Spacecraft are extremely resource constrained so any gains in efficiency that can be made can offer a significant benefit.
